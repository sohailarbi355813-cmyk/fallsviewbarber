About INdustry CUTS

Our Story

Built on Passion, Driven by Excellence

INdustry CUTS opened its doors in 2016 after award-winning stylists David and Marilyn decided to venture out on their own, creating an environment where every client feels at home. They had a vision: to mentor and build a team as hungry and passionate as themselves.

Our team is built from graduates of Niagara College who train through Marilyn and David's intensive programme. Many of them have already become award-winning stylists in their own right — from provincial to international competitions, they continue to put INdustry CUTS on the global map.

Competing gives us the opportunity to work with the best worldwide, keeps us on the cutting edge of trends, and pushes us to deliver a level of craft that you simply won't find at an ordinary barbershop.

The Founders

Meet David & Marilyn

Co-Founder & Master Colourist

David

David is recognized as one of Canada's master colourists — one of only 16 people worldwide to earn Goldwell Colour Masters Status (2014). His work has been published internationally, and he has competed and taught across Europe and North America. His most recent achievement: Gold — Master Colourist of the Year at the 2025 Contessa Awards.

🥇 2025 Contessa — Gold, Master Colourist of the Year 🥇 2024 IBI Awards — Best High Fashion Hair Artistry 🥉 2023 KAO Global Creative Awards — National Bronze 🏆 17 International Competition Awards
Co-Founder & Men's Specialist

Marilyn

Marilyn is an award-winning hairstylist with titles in provincial, national, and international competitions — specializing in men's cutting, styling, and bridal work. She spent years as an educator at Niagara College, giving her first pick of the region's most talented emerging stylists.
