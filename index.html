About Us

Where Precision Meets Craft

INdustry CUTS is Niagara's premier destination for men's grooming and barbershop services. Since 2016, we've built a reputation for excellence — training a team of award-winning barbers who are passionate about their craft.

Founded by award-winning stylists Marilyn and David, our philosophy is simple: every man who walks through our doors deserves a cut that makes him feel unstoppable. We travel, compete, and teach across Europe and North America to stay ahead of every trend.
