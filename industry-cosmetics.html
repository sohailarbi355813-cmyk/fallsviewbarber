The Story

Made for the Clients We Know

The INdustry Styling Collection was built around one simple idea: give our clients the tools to recreate the look we give them — at home, every day. Every product in our line started as a conversation between our barbers and our clients about what actually works.

Our products are made using superior, salon-quality ingredients and manufactured almost entirely in Canada. High quality, easy to use, and specifically designed for men who want their hair to look great with minimal effort.
